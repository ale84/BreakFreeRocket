Break Free Rocket

 

Break Free helps you keep track of days of freedom from bad habits / addictions.

Add a counter for a problematic behaviour you want to get rid off and watch it increase one day at a time, while you focus on becoming a better version of yourself.

Features:

  • Add as many counter as you want for any behaviour you want to track.
  • You can set a goal for a counter, or have it go on indefinitely.
  • Receive notifications when you complete a counter.
  • Track counter progress from your iPhone locked screen with a cool widget.
  • Add notes to counters and relapses to help you identify your weaknesses.
  • Cool animation for completed counters!

Note:

This app is completely free and without any ads.
